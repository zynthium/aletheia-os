# Playbook — Orient

Use at the beginning of any non-trivial AI-agent session.

The assistant must answer:

1. What is the active node?
2. What parent constraints apply?
3. What is the task trying to change: theory, design, evidence, contract, engineering, risk, or optimization?
4. What evidence or state update will be required when done?
5. What would count as invalidation?
6. What local optimization must be avoided?

If the assistant cannot answer, the task should be reframed before execution.
